Our Supply Chain Process

A systematic approach to quality assurance

At Airavat Overseas, we follow a comprehensive supply chain process that ensures the highest quality standards from sourcing to delivery. Our process is designed to maintain product integrity, freshness, and compliance with international regulations.

We work closely with farmers, producers, and logistics partners to create a seamless supply chain that delivers premium products to our global customers. Each step in our process is carefully monitored and documented to ensure traceability and quality assurance.

Explore each stage of our process below to understand how we maintain our commitment to excellence.

1. Sourcing

We carefully select farmers and producers who follow sustainable and ethical farming practices. Our sourcing team visits farms and production facilities to ensure they meet our quality standards and comply with environmental regulations.

Direct farmer relationships
Sustainable farming practices
Fair trade principles

2. Quality Inspection

Our quality control team conducts thorough inspections of all raw materials and products. We test for quality parameters, contaminants, and compliance with international food safety standards using state-of-the-art testing equipment.

Multi-stage quality checks
Advanced testing facilities
Compliance with global standards

3. Processing

Our products are processed in modern facilities that adhere to strict hygiene and safety protocols. We use advanced technology to preserve the natural qualities of our products while ensuring they meet international standards for export.

Modern processing facilities
HACCP-certified processes
Minimal processing techniques

4. Packaging

We use high-quality packaging materials that protect product integrity during transportation and storage. Our packaging is designed to extend shelf life while being environmentally friendly wherever possible.

Food-grade packaging materials
Eco-friendly options available
Custom packaging solutions

5. Storage

Our products are stored in temperature-controlled warehouses that maintain optimal conditions for each product type. We follow strict inventory management practices to ensure product freshness and traceability.

Temperature-controlled facilities
Advanced inventory management
FIFO (First In, First Out) system

6. Logistics & Shipping

We partner with reliable logistics providers to ensure safe and timely delivery of our products worldwide. Our team handles all documentation and compliance requirements for international shipping, making the process seamless for our customers.

Global shipping network
Complete documentation support
Real-time shipment tracking

7. Customer Support

Our commitment doesn't end with delivery. We provide ongoing customer support to address any queries or concerns. Our team is available to assist with product information, reordering, and feedback collection.

Dedicated account managers
Multilingual support team
Regular follow-up and feedback
