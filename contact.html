Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What are your minimum order quantities?

Our minimum order quantities vary depending on the product category. For most agricultural products, we require a minimum order of 1 metric ton. For frozen and dairy products, the minimum order is typically 500 kg. Please contact our sales team for specific product requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including Letter of Credit (L/C), Telegraphic Transfer (T/T), and Advance Payment. The specific payment terms can be discussed during the negotiation process.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on the destination country and shipping method. Typically, sea freight takes 15-45 days, while air freight takes 3-7 days. We provide tracking information for all shipments so you can monitor the progress of your order.

Do you provide samples before ordering?

Yes, we can provide samples for most of our products. Sample costs and shipping fees may apply, which can be adjusted against your first order. Please contact our sales team to request samples.

What certifications do your products have?

Our products are certified by various international standards including ISO 22000:2018, HACCP, Organic Certification, and FSSAI. Specific products may have additional certifications such as Non-GMO, Gluten-Free, or Vegan certifications. We can provide copies of relevant certificates upon request.
